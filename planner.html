How to use the Planner

  • Type a task, choose a priority level, and click Add Task (or press Enter).
  • Click the circle on the left to mark a task as done.
  • Click the ✕ on the right to remove a task permanently.
  • Use the filter buttons to view All, Pending, or Completed tasks.
  • Your tasks are saved automatically in your browser — they'll be here when you return.
